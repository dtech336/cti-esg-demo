Why I'm excited about CTI

A quick note to explain how I think about sustainability data, AI, and this role.

How this demo connects to the internship

This small project is my way of showing how I approach ESG data work: structured, curious, and focused on giving the reviewer a clear next step. The workflow mimics parts of the internship:

  • Validating and stress-testing data to spot inconsistencies and edge cases.
  • Comparing system output to underlying disclosures instead of trusting the model blindly.
  • Summarizing issues in a way that lets someone act quickly and confidently.

The mindset I would bring

  • Impact first. I care about ESG because it shapes real decisions in banks and investment teams.
  • Curious about AI. I enjoy understanding where models fail, not just when they work.
  • Meticulous with details. Data quality is only as strong as the last unchecked edge case.

This demo is just a tiny example. With real CTI workflows, real data and real feedback, I would love to help make your ESG data sourcing even sharper and more reliable.

Thank you

Thank you for taking the time to review my application and this demo. I would be excited to discuss concrete use cases, share how I built this, and hear how I could contribute to CTI's roadmap.
